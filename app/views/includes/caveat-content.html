Your estimate is not a guarantee.

It’s based on current law and pay information from your employer which allows us to make assumptions, taking into account any additions and reductions, to give an approximate estimate.

It includes:

  • an assumed pay increase of 2% each year
  • an assumed inflation figure of 0.5% each year

It does not include:

  • any reduction to your pension due to a ‘Pensions on Divorce’ or ‘Scheme Pays’ debit, or Early Retirement Reduction Buy Out (ERRBO)
  • any additions to your pension due to Additional Pension you may have bought, or benefits you have transferred from another public sector pension CARE scheme
  • tax. Tax rules are applied when you apply to get your pension
